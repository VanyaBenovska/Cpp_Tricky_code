11_02_In_Range_LOWER_BOUND
You are given a line of integer numbers, followed by another line containing exactly two numbers – the start (inclusive) and 
end (exclusive) of a range (start will always be less than end). Write a program that prints all numbers from the first line that fall 
into the range [start, end), in increasing order, without duplicates.

Input1:	
1 42 13 1 13 10 9 7 4 105
7 12	

Output:
7 9 10

Input2:
1 4 7 9 10 13 42 105
Output:   -

Input3:	
1 42 13 1 13 10 9 4 105
7 13	

Output:
9 10
